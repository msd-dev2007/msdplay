Modern Combat 5

Prepare for an unparalleled multiplayer FPS experience as Modern Combat 5 once again sets the benchmark for action-packed shooter games. Steptep onto the battlefield and answer the call of duty in this classic title that revolutionized first-person shooter games with its stunning graphics, formidable arsenal, and exhilarating online multiplayer battles.


A Diverse Arsenal of Classes and Game Modes

Modern Combat is the pioneering mobile FPS where you can select from over 10 distinct classes, each with its own unique attributes and abilities. Dive into the action-packed online warfare or engage in thrilling single-player campaigns as you take on dire situations to save the world.

Modern Combat 5 offers a multitude of game modes to suit your playstyle:

  1. Team Deathmatch: Team up with your allies to eliminate the opposition.

  2. Capture the Flag: Engage in flag-capturing missions to secure victory for your team.

  3. Push the Payload: Work together to escort a payload to its destination while battling the enemy.

  4. Free for All: Showcase your individual skills in an all-out free-for-all showdown.

  5. Battle Royale: Survive and conquer in a battle royale mode where only the strongest will prevail.

Intense Shooting Action and Global Competition

Modern Combat 5 delivers intense shooting action that will put your skills to the test. Climb the leaderboards, whether individually or as part of a team, to prove your prowess as a top marksman.

Execute incredible critical shots on the battlefield, or spectate professional players as they dominate opposing forces in online FPS warfare.

Coordinate your multiplayer strategy and chat with your squad in real-time through the Global and Squad chat, including voice communication. Participate in weekly events to earn free rewards and exciting prizes.

Engaging Mobile Shooter Campaign

The game's enthralling campaign puts you in the shoes of a one-man army, a force to be reckoned with. Defy the odds as you embark on fast-paced story missions taking you from the bustling streets of Tokyo to the historic canals of Venice, facing a variety of challenging first-person shooter scenarios.

Modern Combat 5 boasts exceptional graphics, a captivating soundtrack, and voice performances tailored for a first-person shooter game. Customize and level up more than 10 unique classes, each offering specialized abilities to enhance your shooting skills.

Choose your combat style from a diverse range of classes, including Assault, Heavy, Recon, Sniper, Support, Bounty Hunter, Sapper, Kommander, Marauder, Morph, or Tracker, each offering a distinct gameplay experience.

Master the Art of Modern Combat

Level up by participating in pub matches or by completing campaign missions. Make crucial decisions, execute your ops, and fulfill your duty as a soldier. Unlock higher-tier weapons and expendable items as you gain mastery over lower-tier arms.

Modern Combat 5 is a reminder that the battlefield is a place for warfare, not for mercy. Train rigorously, fight valiantly, and answer the call of duty with unwavering determination when death beckons.



Experience Real Warfare with Modern Combat Style

Modern Combat 5 invites you to embrace the true essence of war. Channel all the fury of battle when faced with adversity.

Immerse yourself in the action and dominate online battles or simply enjoy an exciting match with friends. Please note that an internet connection is required to enjoy the game.

Modern Combat 5 is not just another shooter game; it's an epitome of excellence in the world of mobile gaming, offering unmatched graphics, competitive multiplayer action, and captivating single-player campaigns.
