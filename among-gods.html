Among Gods! RPG Adventure

Prepare to embark on an epic dark fantasy RPG adventure that will test your mettle against Gods and the forces of Evil. In this captivating world, you'll lead your own squad of heroes, uniting with legends from across the land to protect it from impending doom.


The Battle of Light and Darkness

Once, the God of Light stood as the protector of humanity, but now, its power wanes as the God of Darkness ascends.

Darkness creeps across the realm, tempting heroes with its malevolence, and the land teeters on the brink of destruction. You are humanity's last hope, standing side by side with a multitude of legendary heroes from various factions.

Train your own gods, make them your ultimate warriors, and venture forth to conquer the encroaching darkness to become a legendary squad.

Game Features:

Exquisite 3D Graphics

Among Gods offers a visual feast with its remarkable 3D scenes and hero models. The attention to detail in every aspect of the game's design immerses you in a rich RPG adventure. The visceral 3D artwork brings the fantasy world to life, making every battle and exploration truly captivating.

Develop Your Heroes

Ascend your heroes by increasing their star ratings and honing their unique talents. Equip them with potent gear and relics to bolster their abilities.

Through dedicated training and preparation, your heroes will be ready for various battles, ensuring they can face any challenge that comes their way.

Collect 100+ Epic Heroes

Team up with over 100 epic heroes from the annals of godly history. These legends will serve as your ultimate fighters, helping you vanquish enemies and demons to gain experience, valuable gear, rare loot, and special drops. Each hero has their own story and unique abilities, making your roster a diverse and powerful force.

Assemble the Ultimate Squad

Create a formidable team of five heroes to take on numerous challenging PvE bosses. Rank up your heroes to unlock potent Bond buffs and special skills, allowing you to dominate the battlefield with your distinctive combat style. Strategy is key as you craft the perfect squad for each encounter.

Battles Over Dozen Giant Bosses

Summon hundreds of epic heroes through a gacha system in the Tavern and collect their hero images from five distinct factions: Justice, Evil, Neutral, Holy, and Corrupt. Expand your Gallery as you assemble a diverse cast of formidable warriors, each with their own strengths and weaknesses.



Dominate in PvP and PvE

Among Gods offers a wide array of gameplay options to test your skills. Engage in the Story Campaign, embark on the Heroic Crusade, prove your mettle in the Arena, explore the mysterious Stonehenge, take on the Nightmare Challenge, uncover the truth in the Trial of Truth, and challenge formidable Guild Bosses and even dragons.

Rich and varied battlefields await you in both player-versus-environment and player-versus-player scenarios, offering endless opportunities for adventure and glory.

Enter the world of Among Gods and embrace the challenge of battling deities and the forces of evil. Build your legendary team, explore the vast world, and prove your worth as a hero in this dark fantasy RPG adventure!
