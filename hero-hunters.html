Hero Hunters: 3D Shooter War

Prepare for the ultimate online multiplayer hero-based battle royale right on your mobile device with "Hero Hunters." With a roster of over 100 powerful heroes to recruit, this game takes the crown as the #1 in Third-Person Shooting RPG Games. Form alliances with friends and embark on epic battles to secure survival!


Key Features:

  1. Massive Hero Roster: Assemble a formidable team of 100+ heroes, each with their unique abilities and weapons, and venture out on thrilling hunting missions.
  2. Co-op Missions: Team up with friends to take on cooperative missions and challenge players worldwide in real-time multiplayer online PvP battles.
  3. Diverse Gameplay: Engage in various game modes, including Daily Events, Gauntlet Mode, Survival Mode, and epic Boss Raids to keep the action fresh and exciting.
  4. Weapons and Abilities: Wage warfare in stunning 3D battles armed with an arsenal that includes guns, sniper rifles, swords, energy cannons, and more. The tactical use of these modern and futuristic weapons can turn the tide of any battle.
  5. Real-time Hero Switching: Seamlessly switch between your heroes during battle, enabling real-time, team-based combat. This dynamic feature keeps the gameplay exhilarating and strategy-driven.
  6. Special Abilities: Unleash jaw-dropping special abilities that can quickly sway the outcome of battles in your favor.
  7. Hero Collection: Collect legendary heroes, each equipped with their own weapons and unique powers. Create the perfect hero roster to take on your adversaries.
  8. Hero Types: Choose from a diverse range of hero types, including snipers, assault specialists, shotgun wielders, magic users, fantasy heroes, warriors, robots, cyborgs, and more.
  9. Loadout Customization: Perfect your hero loadout by looting after each battle, leveling up your heroes, upgrading abilities, and fine-tuning your arsenal.
  10. Single-Player Campaign: Immerse yourself in the post-apocalyptic cityscape as you engage in modern combat against raiders, bandits, and other threats.
  11. Real-Time PvP Tactical Battles: Challenge your skills against real players in intense, action-packed tactical PvP combat. Craft a squad of up to five heroes, climb the leaderboards, and form alliances to dominate your rivals.

What's New in Version 7.4.1

  1. Altera Update: Fixes have been applied to Ruby Skill for the Altera Hero.
  2. New Hero: A new 5-star hero, Altera, has joined the fray. Altera, a person driven by strong morale and the desire to help others, developed a formula that not only enhanced her physical capabilities but also reversed her aging process. To further perfect her formula, she took on the only job that allowed her to do so, embarking on a journey filled with challenges and heroism.
  3. Special Event: Prepare for the Halloween Scavenger Hunt event, offering a chance to earn a rare+ skin: Prophet.


Hero Hunters redefines the mobile gaming experience by offering a dynamic blend of action, strategy, and camaraderie. Assemble your dream team of heroes, adapt to diverse gameplay modes, and engage in thrilling PvP battles. Dive into the world of Hero Hunters and become a legendary champion!
