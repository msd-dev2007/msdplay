Nexus War: Civilization

Embark on a thrilling sci-fi journey with "Nexus War," a strategic conquest game that catapults you into the depths of Origin Star. As the Astra descended upon this once vibrant world, it left a trail of devastation, reducing cities to unrecognizable ruins. The end is imminent for Origin Star, and every race that calls it home is locked in a desperate battle for survival in this new era of terror.


Destiny has chosen you as the harbinger of hope. Your mission is clear: raise an army, seek out the mightiest heroes from the four races inhabiting Origin Star - The Humans, Izans, Aokus, and Theias. Unite them all to confront the Invaders who threaten your home planet. Your goal is to restore Origin Star to its former glory!

Key Features of Nexus War:

  1. Explore the Mysteries of Origin Star: Traverse the open world and unravel the secrets of Origin Star. Your journey will lead you to relics and hidden treasures scattered across lands teeming with monstrous adversaries.

    Encounter the natives and the four governing races of Origin Star. Navigate through deception and conflicts as you work to either ignite or prevent war. Your ultimate objective is to annihilate the Astra and bring new hope to the survivors.
  2. In-Depth Strategic Battles: Take command of dozens of unlockable heroes from each race. Customize your army by selecting from infantry, artillery, tank units, and more. Lead your forces into uncharted territories and strategize your way to victory.

    Decide whether to outmaneuver your enemies with cunning tactics or overpower them with sheer military strength. Engage in real-time wars that deliver an exhilarating gaming experience.
  3. City Building and Personalization: Shape and design your city using four distinct architectural styles inspired by each race. Create megastructures such as Titan Docks, Research Labs, Intelligence Buildings, and more to construct your city according to your unique vision.
  4. Alliance Battles and Ruler Ascent: Legends speak of the Infinity Throne, the source of absolute control and power over the entire planet. With the unrivaled authority bestowed by the Throne, one can access the rarest resources and technological advantages.

    To ascend the throne and become the ruler of Origin Star, you must face daunting challenges. Hunt the most formidable monsters and defeat the mightiest alliances to earn your right to ascend the throne.

What's New in Version 0.2.133

  1. New Features: "Knock Knock" and "Phantom Legacy" have arrived, introducing fresh content to elevate your gaming experience and expand the story within the Nexus War universe.
  2. Adjustments, Fixes, Optimizations: The development team has been hard at work to ensure a smoother gameplay experience. Notable updates include the addition of tile PVP reports in the alliance log, adjustments to Astra models, the implementation of construction rules for alliance strongholds in S1, and optimizations to address display issues during building upgrades. These enhancements aim to provide you with a seamless and enjoyable gaming journey.


"Nexus War" invites you to partake in an epic adventure where survival, strategy, and exploration converge. Forge alliances, uncover the mysteries of Origin Star, and vie for the ultimate seat of power on the Infinity Throne. The fate of an entire world rests in your hands. Are you ready to rise to the challenge and rewrite the destiny of Origin Star?
