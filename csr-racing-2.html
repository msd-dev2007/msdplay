CSR Racing 2 Realistic Drag Racing

Race your way to the top of the leaderboards in your custom supercar with CSR2! Welcome to the ultimate drag racing experience that fits right in the palm of your hand. CSR2 is a hyper-realistic driving simulator, the third iteration in the acclaimed CSR series, following CSR Racing and CSR Classics. With millions of players worldwide and exclusive partnerships with renowned car manufacturers, CSR Racing 2 offers an unparalleled racing experience for automotive enthusiasts.


Key Features:

Realistic Drag Racing:

CSR2 isn't just a game; it's a real driving simulator that brings the thrill of drag racing to life on your mobile device. The hyper-realistic gameplay, stunning graphics, and extensive car lineup make it a fantastic drag racing experience for all motorized vehicle fans.

Global Competition:

Compete with players from around the world in custom-built cars, including iconic models like the Ferrari SF90 Stradale, McLaren Senna, Bugatti La Voiture Noire, and more. Engage in real-time driving game challenges and join forces with friends to form a crew, fine-tune your rides, and maximize your speed. In the world of free car games, it doesn't get more real than this!

Licensed Vehicles:

Show off your car collection in your expansive warehouse garage. CSR2 features officially licensed vehicles from renowned manufacturers like Porsche, Aston Martin, Lamborghini, Pagani, Koenigsegg, Toyota Supra Aerotop, Nissan Skyline GT-R (R34 NISMO S-tune), Chevrolet Camaro ZL1 1LE NASCAR, and the Mercedes-AMG F1 W11 EQ Performance #44.

Campaign Modes - Elite Tuners and Legends:

Experience thrilling single-player drag races across stunning race courses. Work your way up the ranks, starting from the Junior Dragster category and aiming for Top Fuel by defeating top street racing crews in the city.

Elite Tuners and Legends:

Take your racing to the next level with "Elite Tuners." Explore thousands of car driving simulator and customization options, from engines and tires to rims, traction, clutches, full-body wraps, and more. Unleash your inner racer and add legendary cars like the Toyota GR Supra, Nissan GT-R (R35), and the McLaren F1 in the "Legends" category.

Multi-Mode Gameplay:

CSR2 offers diverse gameplay modes, from speed races against opponents to drag races against friends. Experience the thrill of real racing as you launch from the starting line and burn rubber on tracks across North America and Europe.

Car Customization:

Collect iconic cars from the '60s, '70s, '80s, and even the '90s. Customize your rides to match your style and conquer the hill for the ultimate racing experience. Take your racing skills post-midnight in this free driving game.

City Car Driving:

Feel the rush as you tear up the road, mile after kilometer, in contemporary cars. Experience the real car drift games with augmented reality (AR) mode, putting you in the driver's seat of these high-speed racing cars.

Furious Drifting:

Master the art of oversteering, opposite lock, oversteers, and countersteering to become a drifting games, parking games, and car parking expert. The thrill of precise car control awaits.

No Limits to Tuning:

Customize your hot wheels with a wide range of options, including paint, nitro, wheels, brake calipers, and turbo enhancements. Craft the ultimate drift racing car or a unique and fun car - the choice is yours!

No Wifi, No Problem:

Enjoy offline car games that let you master 9-second cars no matter where you go. There are no boundaries to your racing adventure.

Age Restriction:

Note that players must be 13 or older to enjoy this thrilling car game. While CSR Racing 2 is free to play, it does offer in-app purchases. You can manage and control these purchases by adjusting the settings in your device's "Restrictions" menu.



Secure Your Purchases:

To prevent unauthorized purchases, select "Set or Change PIN" in the Google Play settings menu, create a PIN, and enable the "Use PIN for Purchases" option. This additional security step ensures that you'll need to enter your PIN before completing any transactions.
