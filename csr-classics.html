CSR Classics

Introduction:

Get ready to embark on a journey through automotive history with CSR Classics, brought to you by the creators of CSR Racing! This exceptional drag racing game lets you experience the glory of classic cars from the last 60 years, all on your Android device, and it's completely FREE!


Key Features:

Race Over 50 Iconic Classic Cars:

CSR Classics brings legendary classic cars from the past to life. Take the wheel of more than 50 of the greatest cars ever made, including the iconic Shelby Mustang GT500, Ford GT40, Plymouth Superbird, and classic models from renowned brands like BMW, Chevrolet, Dodge, Ford, Mercedes, Plymouth, Pontiac, and Shelby.

Restore Your Ride:

Transform your classic cars from rusty, neglected shells into stunning symbols of driving excellence. The game allows you to restore these timeless classics to their former glory, making them not just cars but true automotive icons.

Clash of the Titans:

Experience thrilling drag races with epic showdowns between legendary cars. Witness the Cobra vs. Mercedes 300SL, Dodge Superbee vs. Chevrolet Camaro, Ford Mustang vs. Skyline GT-R, and more. It's a battle of automotive titans, and you're in the driver's seat.

Own the City That Never Sleeps:

Take on the ruthless gangs that rule the city streets and face off against the toughest drivers out there. CSR Classics challenges you to prove your racing prowess and assert your dominance. Do you have what it takes to own the streets?

System Requirements:

To enjoy the classic car racing action of CSR Classics on your Android device, you'll need a device with at least 1GB of RAM and Android OS 4.0 and above. Ensure your device meets these requirements for the best gaming experience.

Important Note About In-App Purchases:

CSR Classics is free to play, but it offers in-app purchases that allow you to enhance your gaming experience. These purchases can be made with real money, so exercise caution. To prevent unauthorized purchases, you can set up a PIN. Simply go to the Google Play settings menu, select "Set or Change PIN," create a PIN, and enable the "Use PIN for Purchases" option. This additional layer of security ensures that you must enter your PIN before completing any transaction.

Explore More Racing Adventures:

If you're a fan of drag racing and classic cars, why not take your passion to the next level with CSR Racing 2? It's the latest installment in the #1 drag racing series of all time and promises an even more exciting racing experience.
