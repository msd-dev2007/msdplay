Marsaction: Infinite Ambition

Prepare to embark on a mission of interplanetary significance! The fate of humanity rests in your capable hands as you set out to eliminate the Swarms on the red planet, Mars.

Decades have passed since the Human Union launched the Mars Colonization Program. Over generations, humans have painstakingly built a new home on this enigmatic world, living in harmony with the native inhabitants, a unique insectoid species known as the Swarm.


However, the tranquility was short-lived as the Swarm inexplicably mutated, transforming from once-friendly neighbors into hostile adversaries. The survival of the human race on Mars now faces a grave threat from these primitive creatures. Understanding why the Swarm turned aggressively hostile has become paramount.

General, your mission is clear: set foot on the Martian surface and establish your Base to protect our people. The path ahead is treacherous, a road less traveled, but with strategic acumen and unity with your allies, you can become the stalwart protector of human civilization on this distant planet.

Key Features:

  1. Exploration: Venture into the uncharted territories of Mars, engage the Swarms in battle, and rescue survivors. When your Exploration Progress reaches 100%, you can expand your Base and enhance your power. But tread cautiously, for you may encounter colossal alien sandworms and arachnids.
  2. Alliance: Forge alliances with fellow players from around the world. Here, you can embark on this epic adventure together. Unity is strength, and your alliance members will stand by your side through thick and thin.
  3. Captain: Your Captain is your trusted right-hand person and the leader of your army. Enhance your Captain's skills and craft specialized equipment to gain various advantages in your quest.
  4. Heroes: Recruit Heroes through the Space Capsule and build an elite squad. These Heroes come from diverse backgrounds but share a common understanding of the challenges ahead. They will be your invaluable allies in accomplishing critical missions.
  5. Strategic Planning: Every step on Mars demands meticulous planning. Make informed decisions when constructing buildings and conducting technological research. Don't forget to manufacture the finest Mecha Warriors and deploy them with a clear objective. A brilliant General always finds the path to victory.


Your journey on Mars is one of epic proportions, where you must confront the mysteries behind the Swarm's transformation and ensure the survival of the human race on this distant planet. Are you ready to accept this challenge and secure the future of humanity on Mars?
