Injustice: Gods Among Us

Prepare for an epic showdown as you build a remarkable roster of DC heroes and villains in the electrifying world of "Injustice: Gods Among Us."

This free-to-play collectible card game invites you to create a powerful lineup of characters, abilities, powers, and gear, plunging you into thrilling 3-on-3 touch-based action combat.

Fight with Intensity

Unleash the full potential of your mobile device's touchscreen mechanics as you engage in 3-on-3 action combat.


Swipe, tap, and combine moves to execute devastating combos and charge your power to unleash special attacks and super moves directly inspired by the console version of the game.

Level Up Your Team

Evolve your team by crafting the perfect move set, enhancing powers, and upgrading your characters and gear.

Tailor your card collection to your unique playstyle and put forth your finest team as you engage in battles against an array of DC superheroes and supervillains.

Online Multiplayer Duels

Test your mettle in online multiplayer battles by taking on real opponents. Compete with players from around the globe on leaderboards, and participate in tournaments offering fantastic rewards.

Analyze replays of your past offensive and defensive clashes to refine your skills and team composition. Lead your team to glorious victory!

A Vast Roster of Legends

Collect and embody your cherished DC Comics icons, including Superman, Batman, Wonder Woman, The Joker, Green Arrow, Flash, Bane, Green Lantern, Doomsday, and numerous others.

Every alternate version of these iconic characters comes with a unique set of powers and abilities, delivering a fresh and enthralling battle experience.



Stunning Graphics

Experience best-in-class graphics on your smartphone or tablet, complete with custom animations for each superhero and villain.

Engage in battles across iconic DC Comics locations like Arkham Asylum, the Batcave, The Watchtower, all beautifully rendered in immersive 3D.

Please Note: "Injustice: Gods Among Us" is free to play, though it includes items that can be acquired with real money.
