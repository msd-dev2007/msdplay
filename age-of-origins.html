Age of Origins

In a world consumed by a zombie apocalypse, Age of Origins emerges as a beacon of hope. With over 70 million downloads worldwide, this zombie strategy MMO offers a gripping experience where survival, revival, and prosperity are key to humanity's resurgence. Are you ready to lead the charge and guide civilization back from the brink of doom?


Survival - The Battle for Humanity

As the world plunges into darkness, you are humanity's last hope. Survival hinges on your ability to build massive armies, including human soldiers, powerful machines, and explosive weaponry.

But heroes are the ones who truly make the difference. Recruit unique characters, such as an international spy, a mad scientist, and an honorable sheriff, to lead your army.

Face a multitude of deadly foes, from Mutant Zombies and Zombie Bears to Death Mothers. But here's the twist - the game features a real topography. Strategic expansion across a 3D map, complete with lakes and mountains, can be the deciding factor between life and death.

Revival - Reclaiming and Rebuilding

Clear the zombie hordes block by block to reclaim your city. Rebuild essential structures like hospitals, housing, and farmlands, alongside advanced technology and weapon centers.

Scavenge for valuable loot and rescue refugees to increase prosperity, ensuring the growth of your city. Watch as the world map transforms as you progress, unlocking new Prosperity Phases, each with its own challenges and rewards.

Thrive - Forging Alliances and Dominating

To thrive in Age of Origins, you must forge alliances with real-world players worldwide. Engage in real-time battles, where hundreds of players unite to claim victory.

Prepare for massive clan wars, where territorial control, resource management, and technological advancement are the keys to success. Only one alliance can conquer the capital and determine the future of this new age of humanity.

Commander, do you have what it takes to guide humanity through this treacherous path? The war for life has already begun. Enter Age of Origins and make your mark on this post-apocalyptic world. Your decisions and strategies will shape the future, and only the strongest will prevail.



Take your place in this epic battle. Rebuild civilization and lead humanity to a brighter future in Age of Origins.

Feel free to dive into this thrilling world of survival, strategy, and hope as you navigate the challenges of Age of Origins. Join millions of players in this enthralling journey that puts your leadership skills to the ultimate test. Survive, rebuild, and thrive in the face of a zombie apocalypse.
