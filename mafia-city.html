Mafia City Apk Download

Welcome to the thrilling world where the mafia reigns supreme. In this action-packed strategy game, you'll vie to become the Godfather, mastering wit and time management to claim your empire.


Engage in daring heists, steal from banks, forge alliances with other players, and together, aim to seize control of the city and the entire Mafia underworld. It's a world where the rules are made on the fly, and every decision can be the difference between fortune and failure.

Not only are you immersed in the crime-ridden streets, but you'll also have a crew that has your back. Date cute babes who support your endeavors and heroic crew members who show unwavering loyalty, ensuring you're never alone in your journey to the top.

The game offers a real-time interactive turf war with daily skirmishes that keep the excitement alive year-round. Engage in epic battles as you challenge rival gangs and establish dominance.

What's a Mafia lifestyle without flaunting your riches? Collect luxury cars that not only impress your enemies but also aid you in exhilarating street races. Challenge the police as they try to thwart your latest heists.

Game Features

Immerse yourself in a high-definition world where you can zoom in and manage your own Mafia Turf with stunning graphics. The virtual reality of the game is an experience you can't afford to miss.

This real-time strategy game demands strategic alliances with other players and continuous self-improvement to face stronger opponents. Craft your path to the top of the criminal ladder.

Delve into magnificent technology trees that you must manage to acquire the most potent crew members. Arm yourself with guns, armored vehicles, fearless bikers, sharp knives, and individuals ready to fight with their bare fists.

Explore extensive maps filled with diverse enemies and special buildings that are updated weekly. Daily exploration is crucial on your journey to becoming the Godfather.

Engage in weekly events that celebrate a variety of cultures and keep players on their toes, constantly adapting to new challenges. Personalize your crew leader to become the most feared boss in the city. Lead with authority and establish your reign over the mafia world.



The Four Types of Crew Members You Can Recruit

BULKERS: These human weapons are your first line of defense, capable of protecting your crew and taking down your enemies.

SHOOTERS: Gallant gunners who wield an array of long-ranged weapons, providing valuable firepower for your operations.

BIKERS: These speed demons take to the streets on their bikes, conducting ruthless robberies and leaving fiery chaos in their wake.

MODIFIED VEHICLES: Altered vehicles that dominate the alleys, enabling the transportation of stolen goods and striking fear into your adversaries.
