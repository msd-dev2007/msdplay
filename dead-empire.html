Dead Empire: Zombie War

Prepare to crush the relentless zombie hordes and defeat the Dead Empire in Dead Empire: Zombie War, a real-time war strategy game set in the late modern period.


Immerse yourself in a captivating storyline centered around a group of remarkable and diverse women with unique life experiences. These women unite to aid the Allies in their battle against the invaders and the malevolent zombie forces created by the invaders during the war.

As the Commander, you are tasked with training formidable troops and recruiting beautiful female Officers to lead them. Collaborate with other Commanders to obliterate the Invaders and vanquish the evil zombie forces, ultimately establishing a more robust Guild and achieving world peace.

Key Game Features

1. Brand New Troop Control System

Experience a groundbreaking troop control system that empowers players to command multiple troops with newfound freedom.

You can orchestrate their movements, garrison positions, and alter their targets and marching routes on the battlefield.

In this game, exceptional leadership and strategic thinking are paramount as powerful troops alone cannot ensure victory. Forge your path to glory through tactical brilliance.

2. Vivid War Scenes

Immerse yourself in meticulously crafted cities and battlefields based on actual geographical locations from late modern Europe.

You'll encounter landmarks recognizable to all, and the game brings to life the famous war machines used during this era, transporting you back to a time when legends were born. Engage in epic battles within rich and realistic environments.

3. Real-time Multiplayer Combat

The thrill of facing real players in battle is incomparable. Defeating human opponents requires a level of strategy and cunning that AI-controlled foes cannot match.

Even as a formidable Commander, you'll need the support of other players because the challenges you face may not come from a single adversary. Be prepared to confront entire Guilds and alliances in this dynamic and ever-changing multiplayer landscape.



4. Multiple Countries to Select

Choose your preferred country to represent in the game, each with its unique Country Trait. Take command of historically renowned war machines and combat units specific to your chosen country.

Lead your chosen army and launch formidable offensives against your enemies. The path to domination is open, and your choices will influence the course of history in the game.

Millions of players have already joined this legendary battlefield. It's your opportunity to expand your Guild, showcase your might, and seize control of this land. The fate of the world is in your hands, Commander.
