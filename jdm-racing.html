JDM Racing: Drag & Drift Race

Get ready for a thrilling dose of online street racing and car customization with JDM Racing: Drag & Drift Race. This is not just another racing game; it's a real drag racing simulator that takes the world of Japanese cars to new heights. Featuring extreme drag drift racing, exciting drag car racing events, and a wealth of car tuning options, it's undoubtedly one of the most addicting car tuning games available.


Key Features:

  1. Legendary Japanese Cars: Step into the world of iconic Japanese cars, spanning from the 1980s to the present day. This game brings together a collection of legendary vehicles, making it a haven for Japanese car enthusiasts.
  2. Customization Galore: Dive into the world of car customization with an array of tuning parts to make your ride truly unique. Whether it's about enhancing performance or adding a personal touch to your car, the options are endless.
  3. Beautiful Tracks: Immerse yourself in the thrill of racing on meticulously designed tracks that are perfect for car drift driving games. Train, compete, and emerge victorious in intense drag car racing competitions.
  4. Varied Race Modes: Experience a wide range of race modes, including drag racing, time attack, sprints, checkpoints, survival challenges, and more. Each mode offers a different kind of adrenaline rush, keeping you engaged and excited.
  5. Career Progression: Embark on a career mode where you can gain valuable experience and unlock secret cars. The journey to the top is filled with excitement and rewards.
  6. Realistic Car Experience: Each car in the game is unique, offering a realistic feel of their weight and power. Finding the perfect balance is essential for victory in the races.
  7. Customization Options: Make your cars stand out with a variety of customization choices, including paint, tuning, and rims. It's your chance to create a truly unique ride that represents your style.
  8. Authentic Sound: Enjoy the immersive engine sounds that match every car, making the racing experience more authentic. From turbine to gearbox and tire sounds, every detail is covered.
  9. Control Options: Tailor your gaming experience with controls that suit your style. Whether you prefer buttons, a steering wheel, accelerometer, or a controller, the game offers flexibility.

New Game Modes:

Custom Multiplayer: Get ready for intense custom multiplayer races. Choose your car, select a track, and invite 2 to 6 players from around the world to join the race. Showcase your tuned car and racing skills in exhilarating multiplayer competitions, whether you're racing against friends or random opponents.

Random Multiplayer: Challenge yourself in quick 1-on-1 races with random opponents. Find an opponent within just 15 seconds and win credits for your car upgrades. Victory is the key to success in this fast-paced race battle, making it a thrilling way to enhance your ride.

Experience the future of online racing and car customization with JDM Racing: Drag & Drift Race, created by the same team behind Drift Legends. This game is a cool and realistic drag racing simulator featuring iconic Japanese cars, complete with all the features that car tuning enthusiasts crave. It offers both offline and online racing competitions, allowing you to race against AI-controlled bots and real players from around the globe.



If you're a fan of Gran Turismo-style racing or if you've been captivated by the world of drag drift racing and Initial D cartoons, this game is a must-have for your mobile gaming collection. It combines the thrill of high-speed racing, car customization, and multiplayer competition, making it a perfect choice for racing game enthusiasts.

Don't miss the opportunity to experience the excitement of Japanese cars, drag racing, and intense competition. Download JDM Racing: Drag & Drift Race now and start your journey towards becoming a racing legend in the world of Japanese car enthusiasts!
