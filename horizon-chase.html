Horizon Chase - Arcade Racing

Horizon Chase is more than just a racing game; it's a heartwarming tribute to the golden age of arcade racing. Drawing inspiration from the beloved hits of the 80's and 90's, this addictive racing game is a love letter to all retro racing gamers.

Each twist, each turn, and every lap in Horizon Chase recaptures the essence of classic arcade racing, delivering an unbridled sense of speed and endless fun. Buckle up and get ready for the ride of a lifetime!


Key Features:

  1. 16-Bit Graphics Reinvented: Horizon Chase masterfully revives the nostalgic graphic style of the 16-bit era, blending it with a modern touch. The result is a visual masterpiece that pays homage to the past while embracing contemporary aesthetics. You'll be immersed in the retro racing spirit within a wholly modern experience.
  2. A Tour Through the World's Horizons: This is not just a typical race; it's a journey around the world. As you progress through each new cup, you'll find yourself driving through extraordinary races set against breathtaking backdrops. Watch the sun set, brave rain, snow, volcanic ash, and even intense sandstorms. Whether it's day or night, every track unfolds in picturesque postcards from across the globe.
  3. Senna Forever Expansion Pack: Pay homage to the legendary driver Ayrton Senna with this special expansion pack. It introduces a whole new set of cars, tracks, and features to the game, inspired by the remarkable moments of Senna's career. Relive the excitement and grace of one of Formula 1's greatest drivers.
  4. Barry Leitch, Legendary Soundtrack Composer: Horizon Chase proudly presents the renowned composer Barry Leitch, the genius behind the unforgettable soundtracks of classic arcade racing games. As you dive into the game, you'll be serenaded by Barry's enchanting tunes, perfectly complementing the visual splendor of each horizon. Prepare to be captivated by the harmonious blend of visuals and music.

Horizon Chase invites you to embark on a nostalgic journey, where the exhilaration of classic arcade racing is reimagined for a new generation. The game retains the essence of past racing gems, bringing it to life with modern sophistication. It's a game that not only respects its roots but also celebrates the timeless joy of arcade racing.

As you blaze through each track, you'll feel the rush of speed and the adrenaline of competition. Whether you're cruising under a setting sun, challenging adverse weather conditions, or conquering diverse terrains, every moment in Horizon Chase is a cherished reminder of the glory days of racing games.

And if you're a fan of Ayrton Senna, the Senna Forever Expansion Pack is a must-experience tribute. It lets you step into the shoes of the legendary driver and relive the iconic moments of his career. It's a special nod to a racing hero and a testament to the impact he had on the world of motorsports.



Let's not forget the musical magic woven into the game by Barry Leitch. His compositions add an extra layer of nostalgia and excitement. The music, combined with the timeless visuals, elevates Horizon Chase to a level of gaming where the past meets the present, and it's a joy for the senses.

Horizon Chase is more than a game; it's an experience that invites you to revisit the thrilling days of classic arcade racing. The blend of old-school charm and modern sensibilities makes it an enjoyable ride for gamers of all ages. It's a reminder that the love for retro racing is timeless, and the spirit of classic arcade games lives on!
