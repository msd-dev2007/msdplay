Frost & Flame: King of Avalon

Prepare to embark on a legendary journey in the mystical realm of Avalon. The once-great King Arthur has fallen, a victim of his own nephew Mordred's treachery. His body now rests within a fortress on the sacred Isle of Avalon, alongside his legendary sword, Excalibur.


But the kingdom remains divided, waiting for the day when Excalibur will rise once more, and a new king will be crowned, uniting the empire. The land is torn by strife, cities pitted against each other, each vying for the power and magic of Excalibur, but only one can sit upon the throne...

A Tale of Medieval Fantasy

King of Avalon unfolds in a fantasy medieval England, reviving the timeless legend of King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table.

Your quest begins with building a mighty city, raising a formidable army, and mastering the art of war strategy, essential for intelligent and successful battles. Alongside you stands your faithful dragon, a source of magic and might, as you wage war against your medieval adversaries.

Forge alliances with players from around the world in your quest for survival as the great war between kings looms on the horizon. You are destined to build the most powerful empire the realm has ever seen!

Train Your Dragon and Raise an Army

The path to kingship is paved with challenges. As you embark on your PvP quest to lift Excalibur and ascend to the throne, you'll raise a dragon, building a formidable army to conquer your rivals.

Taste the sweet nectar of power and victory as you make friends and foes on your journey. Chat, trade, assist, and engage in epic battles with players across the globe in this real-time multiplayer adventure. The demise of King Arthur has left the throne vacant, and it's time to embrace the medieval legacy. The epic battle to rule the kingdom with your dragon has begun!

Key Features:

  1. Prepare for War: War is omnipresent. Strengthen your bases, upgrade them, and amass epic armies. You aren't the sole contender for the throne; fierce competition awaits.
  2. Form Alliances: No one can stand alone. Whether you're facing a GvE Barbarian Leader or confronting a formidable PvP adversary, trustworthy allies are your greatest assets.
  3. Spy and Strategize: Send spies to gather intelligence on your adversaries before waging war on their castles. Knowledge is power in this battle for supremacy.
  4. Dragon Power: Dragons, legendary weapons of mass destruction. Train and wield a legendary dragon in the service of your fantasy army, unleashing its immense power.
  5. Global Interaction: The easy-translation feature brings thousands of players from across the world together in real-time multiplayer war, where diplomacy and strategy go hand in hand.
  6. Military Strategy: Research and master the art of attack and defense skills, giving you an edge over your magical adversaries. Timing is crucial; know when to be stealthy and when to launch an all-out assault in this PvP adventure.
  7. Empire Building: Construct the foundations of an empire resilient enough to withstand the fiery clashes of dragon warfare. Manage and produce resources, build your army, conquer the kingdom, and claim the throne.
  8. Epic Adventure: Immerse yourself in the free MMO fantasy adventure and live the legend of Camelot. Battle formidable monsters and harness the power of dragons.

Let strategy be your ally and forge the most powerful army of all time in this multiplayer real-time strategy game. Become the hero of Avalon, a dragon hunter, and rise as the leader of a mighty empire. Lead your army into epic battles and prove your worth in the dragon-fire war!



PLEASE NOTE: King of Avalon is a free MMO for download and play, but in-game items may be purchased for real money. To restrict these purchases, configure password protection in your Google Play Store app. An internet connection is also required to experience this epic multiplayer strategy game.

Are you ready to become the hero of the kingdom army, the King of Avalon, and lead your dragon into battle to become the ruler of an empire? Your adventure in the realm of Frost & Flame awaits!
