The Amazing Spider-Man 2

The Amazing Spider-Man 2 is an action-packed mobile game that plunges players into the thrilling world of the iconic superhero, Spider-Man. In this game, you take on the role of Spider-Man as you swing through the streets of Manhattan, using your incredible abilities to combat crime and protect the city from notorious villains.


Key Features:

  1. Intense Game Experience: Experience the adrenaline-pumping action as Spider-Man takes on criminals with his acrobatic fighting style. Engage in intense aerial combat as you soar through the sky.
  2. Vast Playground: The game offers an open-world Manhattan for you to explore. You have the freedom to traverse the city, assisting those in need and uncovering its secrets.
  3. Enhanced Abilities: Spider-Man's combat abilities have been enhanced, allowing you to launch powerful attacks, defend against enemies, and execute impressive counter-attacks.
  4. Notorious Villains: Face off against famous and formidable villains such as Venom, Green Goblin, and Electro. Your battles with these adversaries add depth and excitement to the gameplay.

Installation Guide:

  1. If you have a previous version of The Amazing Spider-Man 2 installed on your device, start by uninstalling it to avoid conflicts.
  2. Download the latest version of The Amazing Spider-Man 2 APK and OBB files to your mobile device.
  3. Install the "ZArchiver" app, which is essential for managing the game files.
  4. Locate the downloaded APK file and install it on your device. However, do not open the game at this stage.
  5. Return to the download folder and open the OBB file. Click on "Extract" to unpack the game's data files.
  6. Open the "Android" folder, then the "OBB" folder, and click on the "bottom right arrow button" to complete the installation.
  7. Now, your game is ready to play. Enjoy the thrilling adventures of Spider-Man as you swing through the city and fight crime!


Get ready to don the iconic red and blue suit, swing from skyscrapers, and engage in epic battles against some of Marvel's most notorious villains. The Amazing Spider-Man 2 game delivers a true-to-life Manhattan experience with its cutting-edge graphics and action-packed gameplay. Dive into the world of Spider-Man and become the hero that New York City desperately needs.
