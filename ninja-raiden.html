Ninja Raiden Revenge

Prepare to embark on a thrilling journey into the world of Japanese folklore, where legends come to life in the form of the terrifying Orochi. Ninja Raiden Revenge combines the excitement of fast-paced ninja action with elements of hack 'n' slash, RPG, and puzzle adventure. This epic quest will test your skills, courage, and determination as you dive deep into a captivating world filled with danger and mystery.


The Legend of Orochi: Unleash the Eight-Headed Demon

Throughout history, tales of Orochi, a fearsome eight-headed serpent demon with eight tails and a perpetually blood-soaked and fiery body stretching across eight valleys and eight hills, have been shared. Legend has it that Orochi awakens every century, bringing terror to the world with its destructive power.

That dreadful moment has arrived, and Orochi has been resurrected. After laying waste to countless villages, it reaches Wind village, a hidden sanctuary of skilled ninja warriors. Orochi mercilessly defeats all who oppose it, leaving behind only devastation, cries of despair, and an overwhelming thirst for vengeance.

Raiden: The Last Ninja Warrior

Raiden, the last remaining ninja warrior of Wind village, carries the bloodline of Susanoo, the god of storms and the sea who once sealed Orochi. Now, Raiden is chosen to stand against the serpent demon and prevent its reign of terror from spreading further.

His path is clear but treacherous. To save the world and seek retribution for his fallen loved ones, Raiden must embark on a perilous adventure filled with hardships, challenges, and formidable foes.

Key Features of Ninja Raiden Revenge
  1. Action-Packed Gameplay: Ninja Raiden Revenge seamlessly combines the thrill of action, hack 'n' slash combat, RPG progression, and the challenge of puzzle adventures. It offers a rich and diverse gaming experience.
  2. Multiple Environments: Explore eight unique maps containing a total of 96 levels. Each level offers a new set of challenges and enemies, ensuring that the adventure remains engaging and unpredictable.
  3. Epic Boss Battles: Confront eight enormous bosses, each with their own unique abilities, in battles that will push your skills to the limit. Face terrifying foes like zombies and other monstrous creatures.
  4. Intuitive Controls: Ninja Raiden Revenge offers easy-to-master controls, making it accessible to all players. Dive into the action with ease and immerse yourself in Raiden's quest for vengeance.
  5. Character Progression: As you journey through this captivating adventure, upgrade Raiden's skills to unlock new abilities, enhancing his combat prowess and resilience.
  6. Stunning Visuals: Immerse yourself in a world of high-quality graphics that showcase the beauty of Japanese folklore and its mythical creatures.
  7. Challenging Battles: Test your ninja skills and strategic thinking as you face tough adversaries and navigate treacherous terrain. Only the most determined warriors will emerge victorious.


Ninja Raiden Revenge offers a unique gaming experience that weaves together ancient legends, action-packed combat, and challenging puzzles into one epic adventure. You'll find yourself drawn into a world of Japanese folklore, where the fate of humanity rests in the hands of Raiden, the last ninja warrior of Wind village.

If you're ready to become a master warrior ninja, join Raiden on his quest for retribution. Download Ninja Raiden Revenge and brace yourself for an unforgettable journey into the heart of darkness.
