Left to Survive


Life among the dead after an apocalypse on earth. Zombie survival & multiplayer game. Prepare for an action-packed zombie experience! Left To Survive immerses you into a world post-apocalypse where zombies have overrun the Earth, and the battle for survival has begun. experience!


Life has drastically changed, and humanity is struggling to stay alive. The Earth is now under the control of the undead. experience!

Join the fight to save the human race from the zombie apocalypse! Embark on expeditions, meet post-apocalyptic heroes, challenge other players in PvP matches and helicopter raids, and showcase your shooting skills. The game of zombie shooting begins... now!

Features

  1. Fight against Hordes of Zombies: The world is overrun by the undead. There's no place to run or hide. Become the hero of this zombie apocalypse and eliminate all the zombies. Engage in the Survival Campaign, follow the storyline, and free the Earth from the hordes of undead. Help humanity rise from the ashes.
  2. Wide Variety of Armor: On your mission to save the world from zombies, a vast array of weapons and gear are at your disposal. Choose from assault weapons, sniper rifles, machine guns, and shotguns to eliminate zombies quickly. Equip your hero with gear to enhance their skills and powers. Upgrade and evolve weapons and gear to empower your heroes.
  3. Unite All Survivors: Meet the heroes of the zombie apocalypse world, each with unique survival skills and abilities. Upgrade and evolve them to enhance their skills. Gather all the survivors to give them new hope and a new home. These specialists can improve your base and make life easier.
  4. Raid Bases in Apocalypse: Raid enemy bases in this action shooting game. The zombie apocalypse is here, and only the strongest will survive. Raid other bases with your helicopter and collect resources. Be ready for a challenging battle as your opponents' bases are protected. Equip your helicopter with powerful weapons and upgrade it for a successful raid. Fight for survival!
  5. Challenge Other Players in PvP Matches: Test your skills in PvP matches and become the best shooter. It's a chance to challenge other players, gain more shooting experience, and train before facing zombies. Play in 2x2 survival matches or solo. Team up with clanmates and challenge other players during the apocalypse.
  6. Build Your Base for Survival: Build a new home for yourself and other survivors to ensure survival and a brighter future. Make it a safe haven where everyone can find shelter. Produce food and resources, arm your heroes with unique abilities, and modify weapons. Fortify it to protect against raids and looting.
