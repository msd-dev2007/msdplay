Battle of Warships Online

Battle of Warships catapults you into the thrilling world of naval warfare set against the backdrop of World War II.


This legendary mobile game lets you engage in epic sea battles, both online and offline, using a diverse fleet of ships hailing from the era of World War I and World War II.

Your mission: navigate your unique ships, upgrade them to enhance their capabilities, and customize your navy with your country's flag. Daily rewards, skill upgrades, and ship enhancements await as you embark on the quest to become the ultimate God of War on the high seas.

Key Features

  1. Legendary Battleships

    Battle of Warships boasts more than 20 unique battleships, including some of the most renowned vessels from World War I and World War II. Take command of legendary ships like the YAMATO, Missouri, Bismarck, South Dakota, and even an Aircraft Carrier equipped with planes and jets. The choice is yours; become the commander of these iconic warships.

  2. Ship Upgrade System

    Each ship in your fleet has a unique upgrade system, allowing you to enhance its capabilities. Utilize advanced technology and various weapons like torpedoes, interceptors, and missiles to improve your battleship's craft and readiness for warfare. Customize your ships according to your strategic preferences.

  3. Realistic 3D Graphics

    Experience the intensity of naval battles with state-of-the-art 3D graphics. Every detail of the game is designed to reflect the realism of war during WWI and WWII. The battle scenes are meticulously crafted to offer an immersive and visually stunning experience, truly capturing the essence of these historical periods.

  4. Intuitive Controls

    The game offers touch controls that are easy to grasp, making it intuitive to operate your warship. Just position your weapons towards the enemy and hit the fire button to unleash your firepower. Victory is achieved through the art of strategy in the heat of battle.

  5. Dynamic Weather System

    Experience changing weather conditions on the game maps, adding an extra layer of challenge to your naval warfare. Fight under the sun, in the rain, during a dramatic sunset, or even in the midst of a snowstorm. Adapt to the environment and outmaneuver your enemies, just as you would in real military operations.

  6. Massive World Battlefields

    As you embark on your naval missions, you'll navigate through diverse terrains, including mountains and glaciers. These landscapes are your companions in this massive multiplayer game, where you can engage in ocean wars. Battle of Warships is designed for co-op play with friends and is free to download and play on Android.



Battle of Warships offers an immersive experience in naval warfare that transports you to a pivotal era in history.

Whether you're facing off against formidable AI foes or competing with real players, the thrill of commanding legendary battleships and outmaneuvering your opponents is at the heart of the game. Engage in epic sea battles, upgrade your ships, and lead your navy to victory. Victory awaits, Captain!
