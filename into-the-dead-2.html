Into the Dead 2

Prepare for the most intense zombie action shooter in the sequel to the hit game Into the Dead, which has garnered over 70 million downloads worldwide. Enter a world teetering on the brink of a zombie apocalypse where your mission is to save your family at any cost. Arm yourself with a formidable arsenal of weapons and unleash your survival instincts.


Your objective? Maim, mow down, and massacre the undead to keep moving forward. In a world where safety is a distant memory, what are you willing to do to ensure your survival?

Continuing the Nightmare

In Into the Dead 2, the nightmare continues, with exclusive story events that bring fresh terror to your screen. Dive into a terrifying prequel to the iconic Night of the Living Dead and expand your experience to the eerie Ghostbusters universe.

Game Features

  1. Evolving Story and Multiple Endings: Immerse yourself in a gripping narrative spread across 7 action-packed chapters, 60 stages, and hundreds of challenges. Make choices that lead to multiple endings, adding depth to the story.
  2. Powerful Weapons and Ammo Perks: Unlock and upgrade an array of melee weapons, firearms, explosives, and more. Arm yourself to the teeth and stand your ground against the undead hordes.
  3. Varied Gameplay: Adapt to different scenarios - fire from military gun emplacements, take down zombies from atop vehicles, wield melee weapons for up-close encounters, or confront the undead on foot. The choice is yours.
  4. Immersive Environments: Explore diverse locations, from oil fields and military bases to campsites and rural farm communities. Every environment presents unique challenges and opportunities.
  5. Ever-Increasing Zombie Threats: Face a range of zombie hordes, from heavily armored to sprinting zombies. Continuously adjust your tactics to overcome these gruesome foes.
  6. Additional Story Events: Experience five new story events, from blazing forests to frigid mountain peaks. Each event adds a fresh layer of horror.
  7. Daily and Special Event Modes: Test your skills and win exclusive prizes in daily and special event modes. Prove your worth in the deadliest of scenarios.
  8. Loyal Canine Companions: Survive alongside your faithful canine friends. They'll fend off zombies and keep you safe while you venture into the undead-infested world.
  9. Play Offline: No internet connection is required. You can take your zombie-fighting adventure anywhere you go.


Game Permissions

External storage permissions are required to ensure a smooth launch experience for all users. Into the Dead 2 is a free-to-play game but offers in-game items that can be purchased with real money. The game requires permissions to access expansion files for seamless gameplay.
