Guns of Glory Lost Island

Prepare to embark on an epic adventure that may be the defining journey of your lifetime. As old wars continue to rage and new conflicts brew across the globe, adventurers have brought forth the latest news—a mysterious island veiled in an enigmatic fog concealing the secrets of lost ancient civilizations.


It's a treasure trove of lands, resources, treasures, and knowledge, stirring ambitions in the hearts of the greedy. To safeguard your kingdom, the time has come for you to lead an expedition team, delving into the enigma of the Lost Island.

Game Features

Explore the Uncharted Island: Your quest to unearth buried treasures on the cursed island is fraught with peril as you battle ghosts and sea monsters. Vicious pirates lurk in the shadows, ready to strike when you least expect it. Stay vigilant and cautious!

Build Your Stronghold: Venture into the shrouded mists to discover valuable treasures. The tavern is where you can mingle and socialize with adventurers from diverse backgrounds.

Craft Powerful Artifacts: Unearth more treasures and hone advanced skills that will help you overcome the dangers that lie ahead. Your journey forward will be fraught with peril, and the strength of your artifacts will be your salvation.

Form Alliances Across the Globe: Friends and fellow adventurers are your allies, whether you're on land or at sea. No matter how powerful your adversaries may be, you're never alone. Unity is your strength.

Experience Varied Strategies: True kings are resourceful and know how to calculate, maneuver, and manipulate their way to victory. Strategy is your key to survival in this treacherous world.
