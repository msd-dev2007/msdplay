Forward Assault

Prepare yourself for an adrenaline-pumping experience in the world of mobile gaming with Forward Assault. This is not just your average shooter game; it's a first-person Player vs Player (PvP) shooter that combines tactical gameplay, fast-paced action, and stunning graphics to deliver an unparalleled gaming experience.


The Leader of Your Team

Step into the boots of a leader in Forward Assault and prepare to lead your team to victory by taking down the enemy squad. With high-quality graphics and a dynamic, fast-paced gameplay, you'll find yourself immersed in the heart of the action.

An Arsenal of Weapons

Forward Assault offers an impressive array of weaponry, including shotguns, snipers, rifles, assault rifles, and more. It's your choice to pick your weapon of preference. You can either play as a counter-terrorist, working tirelessly to prevent terrorists from planting a dangerous bomb, or take the role of a terrorist, attempting to destroy your primary objective.

Strategic and Tactical Battles

Engage in battles on strategic and tactical maps, where every move counts. Success requires teamwork, strategy, and precise execution. Forward Assault offers a variety of fun and tactical game modes, including:

  1. Ranked Mode: Here, you can play as either a terrorist or counter-terrorist, with the main objective being to plant or defuse a bomb. Winning matches allows you to climb the rank ladder.
  2. Gun Game: A team-based mode where you receive a new weapon with each kill. The first player to reach the knife wins.
  3. Team Deathmatch: The objective is to eliminate as many enemies as possible to earn points. The team with the most points when the timer runs out emerges victorious.
  4. Sniper Team Deathmatch: Similar to Team Deathmatch but with a unique twist – only sniper rifles are allowed.
  5. Infected: In this mode, your team faces off against a zombie, but beware - if the zombie catches you, you become one too. To secure victory, your team must survive until the timer runs out. The game ends if everyone gets infected.

Customization Galore

Forward Assault doesn't just offer thrilling gameplay; it provides a plethora of customization options. Personalize your rifles, submachine guns, pistols, knives, and gloves with eye-catching skins. Stand out from the crowd with a stylish gold tag featuring your name. Adjust your Heads-Up Display (HUD) to your liking and even create custom games to challenge your friends.

Main Features of This Online FPS Marvel

  1. Play as a Counter Terrorist or Terrorist, each with distinct missions and goals.
  2. Choose from an impressive selection of weapons, including guns, rifles, shotguns, knives, assault rifles, and snipers.
  3. Experience fast-paced, tactical shooter gameplay.
  4. Immerse yourself in incredible graphics and dynamic sound effects.
  5. Enjoy real physics and immersive effects, making battles come to life.
  6. Engage in online multiplayer PvP matches, competing against players from around the world.
  7. Become a clan leader and participate in clan wars, tournaments, and scrimmages.
  8. Forward Assault stands as one of the best PvP shooters available on mobile devices.
