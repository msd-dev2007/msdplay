The Ultimate Zombie Game

Are you ready for the most epic zombie game of all time? In Dead Trigger 2, you'll need to rise up and fight for your survival in the midst of a zombie apocalypse. This is not your typical FPS shooter; it's a game that keeps you engaged for months on end.

Your Hideout and Allies

As you embark on this nail-biting adventure, you'll have the chance to build your personal hideout and connect with critical characters like the Gunsmith, Scientist, Smuggler, Medic, and Engineer. Dead Trigger 2 offers more than just zombie shooting; it provides an immersive experience like no other FPS game.

Global Strategy and Diverse Battlefields

Prepare to unlock 10 unique regions and devise a strategy for 33 different battlefields. Your mission: save the world from the clutches of the zombie hordes. Dead Trigger 2 offers diverse landscapes and challenges, ensuring you're always on the edge of your seat.

Compelling Storytelling and Gameplay

Get ready for over 600 gripping gameplay war scenarios and intense storytelling campaigns. Dead Trigger 2's narrative-driven approach sets it apart from other zombie games. Experience a depth of storytelling rarely seen in the genre, keeping you hooked from start to finish.

An Arsenal of Weapons

Dead Trigger 2 boasts more than 70 types of deadly weapons. Never before has killing zombies been this satisfying! Each weapon offers a unique way to annihilate the undead. From traditional firearms to experimental and bizarre weapons, you have the tools you need to survive the zombie onslaught.

Controls That Shine

Dead Trigger 2 gives you the choice between touch controls or an enhanced virtual joystick. We pride ourselves on offering the best FPS controls in the best zombie game, ensuring your gaming experience is seamless.

Brutal Melee Weapons

When it comes to brutal melee combat, Dead Trigger 2 doesn't disappoint. Wield weapons like the Wrench, Bats, Hammers, Katana, Chainsaw, Swords, and Machetes to fend off the relentless undead. In this regard, Dead Trigger 2 stands out as one of the few zombie games with such an array of brutal melee weapons.

Firepower Galore

Choose from a vast array of firearms, including powerful Pistols, Rifles, SMGs, Miniguns, Rocket launchers, Shotguns, and even experimental weapons. Dead Trigger 2 is a shooting game that knows how to deliver an authentic FPS experience.

Unique Gadgets and Weekly Tournaments

Dead Trigger 2 isn't just about zombie killing; it offers an arsenal of fun and entertaining gadgets, from Mines and Turrets to lethal Chickens! Plus, don't miss the weekly Tournaments set in beautiful Arenas, where you can showcase your zombie-killing style.
