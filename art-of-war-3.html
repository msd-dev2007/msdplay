Art of War 3: RTS Strategy Game

Prepare to engage in intense real-time strategy battles like never before with Art of War 3. This mobile game offers a unique experience, setting it apart from other titles in the genre. Are you ready to fight, win, and prove your skills in player-vs-player (PvP) combat?


Art of War 3 is not your typical real-time strategy (RTS) game. It offers direct control for true commanders who aren't afraid to confront their enemies head-on in real-time PvP battles. This is the game for those who crave the thrill of defeating a real opponent.

It's designed for strategists who can make decisive moves in the heat of battle. If you just want to dive into the action and blow things up, this game is not for the faint-hearted.

Are you up for the challenge, Commander?

Art of War 3: Global Conflict, often abbreviated as AOW, is a real-time strategy online game that pays homage to the classic PC RTS games. In this modern warfare game, you'll command, conquer, and defeat your enemies on the battlefield.

Similar to other PvP games you may have played, you'll face off against other players in PvP battles, create new combat tactics, devise victorious strategies, and upgrade your military forces, including infantry, assault vehicles, tanks, navy, and air force, to establish supremacy over your adversaries.

The action unfolds in the near future, where the world is plunged into a global conflict between two rival factions: the Confederation and the Resistance. You must choose your side and join forces with fellow commanders and generals to emerge victorious in this worldwide conflict.

Stand with the Confederation to protect the world from a looming red alert, or align with the rebels to dismantle the global domination system. Become the general of your own formidable strike force.

Key Features:

  1. Epic PvP and Cooperative Battles: Engage in real-time PvP battles and cooperate with thousands of players from all over the world.
  2. Direct Control System: Classic RTS direct control system lets you take command of each unit directly.
  3. Immersive 3D Graphics: Stunningly detailed 3D graphics provide complete immersion in the game world.
  4. Varied Units and Tactical Abilities: Deploy a wide range of units and tactical abilities to craft different victorious strategies.
  5. Two Warring Factions: Choose between the Confederation and the Resistance, each with unique combat units, strengths, and weaknesses.
  6. Unit and Building Upgrades: Numerous upgrade options allow you to create your own distinctive and victorious army.
  7. Continuous World War: Clans battle for global domination in an ongoing World War.
  8. Extensive Campaign: Enjoy a substantial campaign for each faction, providing hours of gameplay.

If you are a fan of PvP and classic real-time strategy games, Art of War 3: Global Conflict is the game you've been searching for. The fate of the world rests on your shoulders, General!

Note: Art of War 3: Global Conflict is a multiplayer online game that requires a stable internet connection.

What's New in Version 3.10.9

  1. Design Updates: The latest version brings design improvements to the confirm action window and nickname change window, enhancing the overall user experience.
  2. Mini-Map Display: Objects on the mini-map in different modes are now displayed correctly, providing players with accurate information during battles.
  3. Tournament Rating: The Blitz Tournament rating no longer displays, streamlining the interface for a smoother gaming experience.
  4. FFA Mode Enhancement: In Free-For-All (FFA) modes, the final battle table now sorts players by rank, making it easier to track your progress and competition.
  5. Daily Reward Fix: An issue where a daily reward box that was already opened continued to be highlighted has been resolved, ensuring accurate rewards distribution.
  6. Premium Daily Rewards: A bug affecting the day counter in the premium daily rewards line has been fixed, ensuring a seamless premium experience for players.


Art of War 3: Global Conflict offers you the opportunity to become a battlefield general. Command your forces, conquer territory, build your base, train infantry, produce assault tanks, armored vehicles, warplanes, and warships to vanquish your adversaries.

Join the World War, utilize superweapons, and unleash your power. Team up with your friends in a clan and take on other factions in the quest for world domination. Fight fiercely and eliminate your enemies completely.
