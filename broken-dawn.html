Broken Dawn: Trauma

Prepare to enter a world shrouded in darkness and despair with "Broken Dawn: Trauma," a gripping campaign expansion of the acclaimed "Broken Dawn." Overnight, humanity found itself on the brink of extinction as mutants of unknown origin swept across the globe.


Cities crumbled one by one, and the survival of the human race hung in the balance. Was this the result of an extraterrestrial invasion, a biological catastrophe, or something even more sinister? In the face of such uncertainty, the truth takes a backseat to the relentless pursuit of survival in these desolate and perilous urban landscapes.

Key Features:

  1. 3D RPG Shooter: Immerse yourself in intense combat, where survival is the only option, in this 3D RPG shooter with polished graphics.
  2. Immersive Controls: Experience a user-friendly control system that enhances your immersion in the game, allowing you to navigate the treacherous post-apocalyptic terrain with ease.
  3. A World in Ruins: Traverse the remains of once-thriving cities, now filled with danger and uncertainty at every turn. The atmosphere of despair and impending doom is palpable as you journey through these dead urban landscapes.
  4. Survival Instincts: To endure in this hostile world, you'll need quick reflexes, keen instincts, and the ability to make split-second decisions that could mean the difference between life and death.
  5. Uncover the Truth: The question of what led to the emergence of these mutants and the collapse of civilization looms large. Delve deep into the mysteries that shroud this dark narrative as you progress through the game.
  6. Challenge the Unknown: Face a variety of threats, from mutated creatures to treacherous human foes. Adapt and strategize to overcome the countless dangers that lie in wait.
  7. Character Progression: Enhance your character's abilities and arm yourself with an array of weapons to stand a fighting chance against the encroaching darkness.

What's New in Version 1.8.1

  1. Improved In-Game Latency: Experience smoother gameplay with enhanced in-game latency. Your actions will be more responsive as you navigate the treacherous world of "Broken Dawn: Trauma."
  2. Issue Correction: A significant issue causing game crashes has been addressed and rectified, ensuring a more stable and enjoyable gaming experience.
  3. Minor Issue Resolutions: Various minor issues that may have impacted gameplay have been resolved, further refining the overall experience.


"Broken Dawn: Trauma" invites you to step into a dystopian world where the line between survival and extinction is razor-thin. With immersive controls, intense combat, and a narrative rich in mystery, this game challenges your instincts and resourcefulness at every turn. Face unknown horrors, uncover the secrets that led to this catastrophe, and strive to endure in a world forever changed by the trauma of an unknown origin.

Are you prepared to take on the challenges of this post-apocalyptic journey and discover the truth hidden within the shadows of "Broken Dawn: Trauma"?
