Defense Legend 4: Sci-fi TD

Prepare to step into the next thrilling installment of the acclaimed Defense Legend series with Defense Legend 4. This game promises players an array of captivating new experiences, delivering an intensified battle against the formidable Dark Force.


In this latest chapter, both our defense systems and modern equipment have undergone substantial development, while the Dark Force has returned with renewed strength and cruelty.

Brief Storyline:

The war between humanity and the Dark Force has evolved into a fierce and unrelenting phase in Defense Legend 4. In previous episodes, humanity discovered ways to thwart and defeat the Dark's relentless assaults.

However, the Dark Force, with its cunning schemes, consistently adapts and evolves after each setback. The battles in Defense Legend 4 present a formidable challenge for followers of the Defense Legend series.

The Dark Force will launch a massive and powerful assault on our strategic bases, attacking from all fronts, be it from the air, land, or even underground. With the addition of remarkably advanced weaponry, the Dark Force has harnessed magical enhancements to amplify their destructive power, posing a significant threat to humanity's very existence.

Key Features:

  1. Intense Strategy: Defense Legend 4 demands players to exercise keen focus and employ effective strategies to repel the relentless Dark Force troops.
  2. Heroic Duo: Two heroes engage the enemy directly, backed by a superhero team equipped with the latest in modern technology.
  3. Cannon Upgrades: Strengthen your defenses by upgrading your cannons and developing special skills to fend off the Dark Force's onslaught.
  4. Diverse Terrains: Engage in battles across various terrains, including deserts, forests, and icy lands, each presenting unique challenges and tactical opportunities.
  5. Varied Strategies: Employ a wide range of strategies, utilizing air forces, ground units, and underground defenses to counter the Dark Force's multifaceted assaults.
  6. Multiple Modes: Dive into the game's numerous modes, offering players a diverse array of challenges and scenarios to keep the gameplay fresh and exciting.


As you take on the role of defending against the formidable Dark Force, Defense Legend 4 provides an immersive gaming experience that requires not only sharp wits but also the ability to adapt to changing battle conditions. With the odds stacked against you, it's up to your strategic prowess to thwart the Dark's merciless advance and protect the world from impending doom.

Get ready to face the ultimate test of your defensive abilities and lead humanity to victory against the relentless Dark Force. The fate of the world hangs in the balance. Will you rise to the challenge?
