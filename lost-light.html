Lost Light

Join the Firefly Squad on a mission to save the world of Lost Light, navigating a post-apocalyptic landscape fraught with danger and mystery. Salvage resources through exploration and battles, face the perils of the Exclusion Zone, and uncover the truth behind the Pheromone Outbreak.


Game Features

  1. The Immersive Apocalypse Battlefield: Survival depends on your knowledge of weapons, map familiarity, hunger and injury management, and strategic battle skills.
  2. Fully Customizable Weapons: With realistic structures and textures, Lost Light offers an immersive shooting experience. The modification system allows for 12 components and over 100 parts, providing endless possibilities for creating your ideal weapon build.
  3. Acting after Planning Is the Key to Survival: Choose between a full build or a lighter approach, and strategize to outsmart enemies. Looting and evacuating without defeating enemies is a viable option, but failure to evacuate means losing everything collected.
  4. Prepare Your Strategies: Being fully prepared in your Shelter is essential. Maintain physical condition, upgrade equipment, and coordinate personalized gear. Smart Pets assist in carrying items or retrieving them if you're defeated.
  5. Diverse Social Interactions: On the battlefield, alliances shift. Choose to rescue, team up, or evacuate with other players. An SOS signal reveals your location if knocked out, but approaching players may be friends or foes.
  6. Cross-platform Feature, FREE on PC: Experience an immersive battlefield on both PC and mobile. Real-time chat and seamless cross-platform functionality enable collaboration between mobile and PC players, unlocking more challenges and goals.

Embark on a journey where survival, warfighting, and progression intertwine. Play Lost Light on PC and mobile for a truly captivating and cross-platform gaming experience.
