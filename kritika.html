Kritika: The White Knights

Embark on a thrilling adventure in the world of Kritika: The White Knights! This is where adrenaline meets your fingertips, offering an action RPG experience that will leave you electrified. Get ready for intense action, exhilarating skills, and the option to call upon a friend's Striker to dominate your foes.


Meet the Diverse Champions

In Kritika, you'll encounter a roster of champions with unique abilities and playstyles. Whether you prefer Rang's dual-wielding attacks or the elemental magic of Elemental Fairy, there's a champion to suit your taste. Each champion has their own story and skills, making the game a rich and engaging experience.

Experience Unparalleled Gameplay

The game offers a game system like no other. Explore the new Guild system, engage in battles with the help of your loyal pets, and grow stronger by conquering the 103 different stages in Stage Mode.

Challenge the Monster Wave, an endless stream of enemies, and ascend the 50 floors of the Tower of Tribulation. Test your skills in Arena, Versus, and Melee, striving to become the ultimate champion.

Defeat Powerful Bosses and Team Up

Take on powerful bosses in the "World Boss" mode, where you can team up with other players for epic battles. The game encourages collaboration, offering a more fun experience when you work together. And with the Auto-Battle feature, you can clear stages more conveniently than ever.



Minimum Device Requirement: Galaxy S4

Kritika: The White Knights is available in multiple languages, including 한국어, English, 日本語, 中文简体, 中文繁體, Deutsch, Français, Español, Português, Русский, Vietnam, and ไทย. Please note that while the game is free to play and download, there may be additional costs associated with acquiring certain in-game items.
