Last Shelter: Survival

The Zombies are closing in, and the last shelter is on the brink of destruction. It's up to you to rebuild and survive in this post-apocalyptic world where every choice you make can mean the difference between survival and becoming one of the undead.


Unique Features

  1. Exterminate Zombies with Towers

    Zombies are at the gate! Your secret weapon, the Defense Tower, is your only hope to weather the crisis. Build fortresses and upgrade cannon towers to annihilate the approaching undead. The fate of the survivors rests on your shoulders.

  2. World Wide War

    Battle enemies from around the globe, lead your Empire to greatness, and fight relentlessly until the last man stands. It's a world in chaos, and your empire's survival is at stake.

  3. Realistic Graphics

    Every detail, from the units and maps to the heroes, immerses you in a complete post-apocalyptic experience. The stunningly realistic graphics bring this desolate world to life.

  4. Build Your Wasteland Empire

    Engage in city building, upgrade facilities, conduct research and development, train warriors and survivors, and recruit powerful heroes – all in the pursuit of living to see another day and conquering the new world that has emerged from the ashes.

  5. Hero System

    Whether you prefer to attack from a distance, defend at close quarters, or focus on base development and farming, a plethora of heroes are at your disposal to assist in your journey. Your choices of heroes will greatly impact your survival strategy.

  6. Strategic Gameplay

    This is not a world where a single type of unit can conquer all. Warriors, Shooters, and Vehicles each have their roles. Understanding your enemy and yourself is crucial to navigate this World War Z-style wasteland successfully. Strategic thinking is the key to survival.



Alliance Warfare

Whether you're battling against other servers or competing for the prestigious title of President in your alliance, your comrades will always have your back. Finding the right people for your alliance is essential for mutual support and success.
